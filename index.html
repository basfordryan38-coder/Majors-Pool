Rulebook

How the pool works

Overview

Each player builds a team of golfers for each major using a 15-point budget. The goal is the lowest cumulative score to par across your team. Each major is independent — there is no season-long carryover.

```

Team Construction

  • You must pick a minimum of 4 golfers and a maximum of 5 golfers.
  • Your total point cost cannot exceed 15 points.
  • Multiple pool players may pick the same golfer — no exclusivity.
  • Golfer costs are based on World Golf Rankings (WGR) the week of the major.

Scoring

Your team score is the cumulative score to par of all your golfers. Lower is better.

Cut Penalty

If one of your golfers misses the cut, they do not stop scoring — they are penalized as follows:

  • Take the worst round score on Saturday (from all players that day) and add it to your golfer's total.
  • Take the worst round score on Sunday and add it again.

Example: Your golfer is cut at +2. Saturday's worst round is +3, Sunday's worst is +5. Your golfer's final score is +2 + 3 + 5 = +10.

U.S. Open Amateur Pick

For the U.S. Open only, each player gets one free amateur pick (does not cost any points).

  • If the amateur misses the cut: no penalty. The pick is simply ignored.
  • If the amateur makes the cut: your team automatically receives −3 strokes.
```
