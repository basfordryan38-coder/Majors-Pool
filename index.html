How the pool works

Overview

Each player builds a team of golfers using a 15-point budget. Lowest cumulative score to par wins.

Team Construction

  • Pick 4–5 golfers, max 15 points total.
  • Costs based on World Golf Rankings (WGR) the week of the major.
